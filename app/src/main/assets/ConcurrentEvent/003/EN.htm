Speakers introduction:
Mr. Thorsten Kuehmann
Managing Director, VDMA Plastics & Rubber Machinery
Thorsten Kuehmann is Managing Director of VDMA Plastics and Rubber Machinery. He has been in the machinery business for 16 years. As Secretary General of EUROMAP he manages the operations of the European association for plastics and rubber machines.
Dr. Reinhard Schiffers
Head of Machine Technology, KraussMaffei Technologies GmbH
After studying general mechanical engineering, Reinhard Schiffers completed his Ph.D. on ‘Process capability in injection molding’ under Prof. Dr.-Ing. Wortbergat, the chair for ‘Design and plastics machinery’ at the University of Duisburg-Essen. Parallel to his activity for the professorial chair, he studied business economics at the FernUniversität (Open University) in Hagen.
After completing his Ph.D. in 2009, Reinhard Schiffers joined KraussMaffei Technologies GmbH in Munich, assuming responsibility for ‘Energy efficiency – BluePower’ within the KraussMaffei Group.
Since 2012 he has been Head of the Machine Technology department within R&D, where his activities cover various areas including: development of hydraulics systems, development of plasticizing systems, measuring technology, pre-development.
Mr. Paul Kapeller
Product Manager Digital Solutions, ENGEL Machinery Co., Ltd.
Paul Kapeller’s education and profession is dedicated to innovation. He specialized in innovation management as he graduated in international business in Vienna and in general management in Linz, Upper Austria. For ENGEL, Paul Kapeller has been working since 2015. Focusing on data-driven services at the beginning, he is currently responsible for the whole portfolio of digital products and services. His commitment to providing tangible benefits with digital technology is bringing both ENGEL and its customers to the leading edge whilst also makes it possible to increasingly leverage the full potential of injection molding.
Mr. Philipp Kremer
Business Development Manager Plastic, KUKA AG
  • 16 years experience in robotic automation
  • 14 years KUKA experience
  • 10 years experience in robot- and PLC programming, technical support and trainings
  • 4 years experience in Product Management
Since March 2016 Global Business Development Manager Plastic
Mr. James Yao
Aerospace & Automotive Vertical Sales Manager, Stratasys Shanghai Co., Ltd.
Graduated from Shanghai Jiaotong University, Information and control system, James Yao has worked as Sales Manager, Shanghai GM of Trimech Technology; Senior sales of 3M, Industry tape and adhesive; Asia Pacific Sales Manager of Cummins Filtration. Since 2014, he has joined Stratasys Shanghai Co., Ltd., been working as Aerospace & Automotive Vertical Sales Manager.
16 years of manufacture solution experience, James is now responsible of Stratasys China direct digital manufacture business.
Mr. Carl Litherland,
Head of Group Marketing, Motan Holding GmbH
Carl Litherland is the third generation of his family to be working in plastics and has more than 25 years of experience in the development of automated raw materials management systems for the plastics manufacturing and processing industries. He is head of group marketing at motan and motan-colortronic.
Mr. Andrea Carta
Director Sales Overseas, Arburg GmbH + Co KG
Andrea Carta was born in Italy and studied mechanical engineering, majoring in plastics processing, at Bielefeld University of Applied Science in Germany. For eight years, he worked internationally as a consultant in the field of supply chain management, before switching to an automotive industry supplier. Here, he worked in Italy and India, as well as spending five years in China as Vice President Business Development Asia. Since November 2013, Andrea Carta has been responsible for all of the Arburg subsidiaries and organisations outside Europe in his function as Director Sales Overseas.
Mr. Gerald Plöchl
Product Manager, Wittmann Battenfeld GmbH
Ing. Gerald Plöchl: graduated from the school of higher technical education in Vienna, with a special emphasis on plastics engineering. He has over fifteen years of experience in the tool making industry, covering all aspects; sales, design and project management.
Gerald Plöchl’s CV includes several years as production manager of the Austrian subsidiary of a UK medical mold-maker & molding company. Since 2005 he has been working for WITTMANN BATTENFELD in various positions, including project engineering, automation and sales.
Gerald Plöchl is currently responsible for medical and cleanroom product management and is the group leader for the company’s Medical & Micromolding department.
Dr. Li Pengzhong
Professor, Sino-German College Applied Sciences of Tongji University (CDHAW)
Dr. Li Pengzhong, Professor of Tongji University, Chief Engineer of MAG manufacturing Technology Chair of Sino-German School of Postgraduate Studies, a senior visiting scholar of Technical University of Berlin and Ruhr University Bochum. His main research fields include manufacturing system optimization and high efficiency intelligent manufacturing technology. The current research interests are focused on development and implementation of Industry 4.0 key technologies. He has working experience in consulting and implementation of a number of well-known enterprises.
Mr. Anston Tan
Principal, German Training Centre for Injection Moulding
Anston graduated from Singapore's Nanyang Technological University has been in the plastic industry since 1999. He co-founded the German Training Centre for Injection Moulding (Asia) in 2014, which brings to Asia from Germany highly valuable and innovative courses for the plastics industry. As principal, he not only localised and adapted the courses to suit the diverse Asian markets, but also motivated industry leaders, educators, and influencers to revitalise and attract talents.
In 2015, Anston further brings to Asia the German concept of ‘Industrie 4.0’ – the fourth industrial revolution after water/steam, mass production, and automation. Today, Anston leads a global team of professionals to help Asian plastics moulders and institutions prepare for the coming era of digitised manufacturing, and he is recognised by many to be one of the foremost authority in this area.
Mr. Ma Yuanye
Operation Director of Intelligent Research Institute and IE Director of White Goods Group, Haier
Ma Yuanye, as Haier’s IE Director, Operation Director of the Research Institute, and expert in industrial engineering and smart manufacturing, has long engaged in promoting Industry 4.0 to manufacturers. He has worked on overall IE business for multiple Foxconn industries and planned the establishment of Haier’s connected factory.

Ralf Pampus is Managing Director of Reifenhäuser China and Director Sales and authorized representative of Reifenhäuser Blown Film in Germany. Mr. Josef Haslinger, Head of Sales China
Brückner Maschinenbau GmbH & Co. KG
Mr. Josef Haslinger is working with Brückner Maschinenbau since 16 years and is the responsible Head of Sales for the Chinese market. Josef’s degree is mechanical and process engineer and he has more than 25 years experience in the field of plastic industry. He started 1989 as R&D engineer with Lenzing in Austria. After 11 years Josef joined Krauss Maffei as development engineer for PVC sheet, film and profile extrusion before he entered the Brückner sales team in 2002. China is Brückner’s largest single market and Josef as Head of Sales is part of this success story.
Mr. Zhou Housong, Senior Engineer

Overall, he has more than 30 years experience in the field of bi-oriented film production (BOPET, BOPP and BOPA).
Mr. Iacopo Bianconcini
Marketing Manager of Packaging Division, SACMI
Mr. Iacopo Bianconcini joined SACMI Group in Imola, Italy, 16 years ago. He graduated in Management Engineering at Greenwich University in London and Master Educated in Key Account Management and Marketing at SDA Bocconi of Milan and Columbia University of NY.
As a Manager in SACMI Imola Headquarter, he is directly responsible for worldwide marketing activities within the Packaging Division, which is grouping both the Beverage and Closures&Containers Business Units.
