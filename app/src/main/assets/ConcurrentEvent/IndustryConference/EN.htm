Route to A0-02 Conference Room

From Outside
If you are near North entrance or West entrace, we suggest you:
From Songze Avenue, North Square: Walk along outside Hall 3H toward West Gate (shown on the map) to reach the Office Building A and take the elevator to Meeting Room A0-02, Level 3.
From Zhuguang Road, West Gate or East Xujing Metro Station Exit 5: Walk along Hall 4.1H (shown on the map) to reach the Office Building A and take the elevator to Meeting Room A0-02, Level 3.
By Car: Parking lots are available at the South Square and North Square, NECC.
By Taxi: Take a taxi to Gate 8, NECC, you will see the entrance of Office Building A.
From Inside
If you are near South entrance or East entrance or inside exhibition hall, we suggest you:
Walk to the 8-meter-high elevated circular tower, then walk along the Exhibition Boulevard between Hall 3H and Hall 4.1H (shown on the map) to reach Office Building A and take the elevator to Meeting Room A0-02, Level 3.
