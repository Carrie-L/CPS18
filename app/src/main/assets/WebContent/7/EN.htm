By Metro
Take Line 2 and get off at East Xujing Station. Walk to West Entrance of NECC by Exit 4, 5 or 6. Or take Line 17 and get off at Zhugang Road Station. Walk to North Hall of NECC by Exit 2.
By Maglev Line
Take Maglev to Longyang Road Station and interchange to Metro Line 2 and get off at East Xujing Station.
By Train
Shanghai Hongqiao Railway Station Take Metro Line 2 and get off at East Xujing Station.
Shanghai Railway Station Take Metro Line 1 to Renminguangchang / People's Square Station, change to Line 2 and get off at East Xujing Station.
Shanghai South Railway Station Take Metro Line 1 to Renminguangchang / People's Square Station, change to Line 2 and get off at East Xujing Station.
